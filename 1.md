# PIT SOÀN Ê CHHO͘ HA̍K

Sǹg-siàu ê hoatto͘ m̄-nā chi̍t-ê. Kiám-chhái beh o̍h sǹg-siàu ê chho͘-ha̍k tiāⁿ-tiāⁿ chiū ēng sǹg-pôaⁿ khiok ōe-chòe tit, nā-sī beh káng-kiù sò͘-ha̍k khah chhim ê lí, tio̍h kia̍h-pit lâi-siá chiah ōe.

Sng3-siau3 e5 huat-too7 m- na7 tsit8-e5. Kiam2-tshai2 beh oh8 sng3-siau3 e5 tshoo-hak8 tiann7-tiann7 tsiu7 ing7 sng3-puann5 khiok ue7-tsue3 tit, na7-si7 beh kang2-kiu3 soo3-hak8 khah tshim e5 li2, tioh8 kiah8-pit lai5-sia2 tsiah ue7.
Tsiang-Tsuan5 tsing3-lang5 soo2-sng3-e5, kan-ta si7 tsinn5-gun5 hit-ho7 siau3, tsiu3 soo2-bat-e5, kan-ta si7 sng3-puann5 e5 huat-too7 tiann7-tiann7, soo2-i2 pîng-sî kio3 tsit-e5 huat-too7 tsue3 Suan3-huat. Kiah8-pit lai5-sia2 ia7 tsin-tsiann3 si7 suan3-huat, tsong2-si7, ai3 hun-piat i, thang kio3-tsue3 Pit-Suan3.
Beh oh8 tsit-e5 Pit-Suan3 e5 huat-too7 tioh8 tui3 tshoo-hak8 khi2, tsiah thang tsiam7-tsiam7 kau3 Tui3-soo3 Tai7-soo3, Sam-kak-huat, kap soo3-hak8 hiah-e5 khah tshim e5 hong-huat. Tsiong tsit-pun2 tshoo7-hak8 hoo7 hak8-sing oh8, i3-su3 m7-si7 beh hoo7 in, oh8 liau2, thang kong2: “Tann bing5-pik8 lah”!si7 beh hoo7 in lioh8-lioh8-a2 tsai soo3-hak8 e5 i3-bi7 tiann7-tiann7, ng3-bang7 lâng-lâng ue7 him-boo7 koh tsit-pun2 khah-tshim-e5.
